Smart Shopping List

This is a simple yet very usefull app that can help you organize your shopping. Users can add categories that represent stores in which they will buy items. Categories and item scan be edited and deleted. Cicking on a text of an item will gray it out and strike it through with a line.

  • The app has 4 activities
  • SQLite database with Room persistance library
  • Made in MVVM arcitecture that is flexible and easy to maintain and to extend.
  • Log in activity that stores users email and password with Firebase.

Future ideas for the app:

  • Sharing lists with other users.
  • Long press an item alows to change order of items.
  • Alows users to see finished and deleted tasks.
  • Alows users to add price tag to an item.
  • List suggests where to buy an item based on price. For example, if an item is cheper in another store the app will throw an alert box where you can choose to put item in another category that represents the store where it is cheaper to buy the item.
