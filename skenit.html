Skenit

The Skenit program is designed for all trainers and fitness centers who want to automate and streamline everyday tasks after realizing the drawbacks and issues associated with utilizing different tools to track exercisers' actions and plan training.

Skenit not only assists you in bettering your business, but it also increases trainee dedication and engagement. Your exercisers receive a user application that, in a novel way, encourages them to work out more and gives them the chance to keep track of their activities, membership costs, and development.

  • For fitness trainers - Helps you manage your trainees
  • For gym admins - Helps you manage trainers and trainees in the gym you are working at
  • For gym owners - Gives you insight in all of the gyms that you own, admins of gyms, trainers and trainees. It also gives you statistics and information about income per employee, gym location and training program.
